What have we done?
OMEN.ETH
Omen is a fully decentralized multi-chain prediction market platform built on the Gnosis conditional token framework. Users can create and participate in information markets on various topics (crypto, politics, sports, etc.) and stake their crypto on possible outcomes.

Envisionists participated in a collaborative visual and functional overhaul of the product, including but not limited to UI and UX, tokenomics, landing page, community framework and marketing materials such as a fully composed informational video, product mascot, social campaigns and more.

Infinite Hackathons
Infinite Hackathons, conceived by passionate decentralization enthusiasts, hosted a uniquely tailored hackathon with a hackers-first approach. The event was a resounding success, providing a space for learning, creating, and innovating, unfettered by superfluous distractions.

Envisionists lead the entirety of the project on behalf of its primary client DXdao. This included event organization, branding, social campaigns, partner acquisition and physical items such as merchandise, banners, entry wristbands, and more.
Operation Decentralization
Operation Decentralization, or OPDE for short, was a podcast project lead by the Envisionists on behalf of DXdao. The project aimed to communicate widely the benefits to decentralization, and demonstrate how DXdao was a pioneer of various decentralized technologies.

Envisionists lead the entirety of the project, including branding, design, scheduling, scripting, and editing.
SWAPR.ETH
Swapr was a DEX, or decentralized exchange, that aimed to hold decentralization to a higher standard than its competitors. By hosting its front-end through IPFS and ENS, Swapr could exist in perpetuity.

Envisionists collaborated with the Swapr product team on a variety of marketing and design tasks, including but not limited to landing page, UI and UX, tokenomics, community tooling, and marketing campaigns such as a fully composed informational product video, and more.

Project DAVI
Project DAVI was the genesis brand for the product team tackling governance at DXdao, including DAO frameworking and social tooling. The team has since independantly transitioned to Outposts, who continue to work alongside the Envisionists today.

Envisionists had a minor engagement with the DAVI team, focusing on branding, copytext, community management, SEO and more.
DXdao
DXdao was a DAO, or decentralized autonomous organization, that owned and operated a variety of products that focused on distributed technologies, many of which are listed in our portfolio!

Envisionists, under the alias "DXvoice", lead "non-technical" operations, included marketing, design, communications, and community management, but the scope of the engagement often overlapped with Bizdev, HR, and governance processes.
Carrot
Carrot is an on-chain incentivization platform tied to real world conditions and outcomes. It aims to incentivize capital efficiency and drive actions that deliver specific results.

Envisionists participated early in product development including but not limited to branding, UI and UX, landing page delivery, physical swag, community frameworking, and marketing materials.
